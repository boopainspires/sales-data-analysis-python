# Sales Data Analysis Summary

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze sales data and find useful business insights.

## Dataset
The dataset contains 10 sales records with these columns:

- OrderID
- Date
- Product
- Category
- Quantity
- Price
- Customer
- City

## Analysis Performed
The analysis answers these questions:

1. What is the total revenue?
2. Which product generated the highest revenue?
3. Which category generated the highest revenue?
4. Which city generated the highest revenue?
5. Which customer spent the most money?

## Key Results
- Total revenue: 2865
- Best product: Laptop with revenue 1600
- Best category: Electronics with revenue 1875
- Best city: Chicago with revenue 1250
- Top customer: Alice with spending 1175

## Insights
- Laptop is the strongest product by revenue.
- Electronics performed better than Furniture.
- Chicago generated the highest sales revenue.
- Alice is the most valuable customer in this dataset.
